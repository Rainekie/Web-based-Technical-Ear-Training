Gain Change - ABX method

This technical ear training develops the ability of audio engineers to distinguish between changes in loudness and changes in frequency during stimuli.

You will now listen to a short piece of music three times. Either the first sound source (A) or the second (B) will be presented with loudness controlled. Then the last sound source (X) will be presented either controlled or uncontrolled, and you will answer whether the sound source for X was A or B.

First, choose stimuli condition below and press the start button. Immediately after that, music will be played three times. Next, select the bottom radio button A or B, and finally press the Send button. The answer will then be displayed.
