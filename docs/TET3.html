EQ Change

This technical ear training develops the ability of audio engineers to distinguish between changes in loudness and changes in frequency during stimuli.

You will now hear a short piece of music or noise twice. The first stimulus (A) will be played without any modulation, whereas the second stimulus (B) will be presented with a modulated frequency. You are to answer where the frequency of the B stimulus has been modulated compared to the A stimulus.

First, you choose the type of stimulus and the gain level. Then, press the start button and the stimulus will flow. You can see your answer by selecting your answer from the choices below and pressing the submit button.
