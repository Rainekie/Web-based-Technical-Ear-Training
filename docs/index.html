Web-based Technical Ear Training — KAIST Edition

version: alpha

This technical ear training develops the ability of audio engineers to distinguish between changes in loudness and changes in frequency during stimuli.

You will now listen to a short piece of music three times. Either the first sound source (A) or the second (B) will be presented with loudness or frequency modulated. Then the last sound source (X) will be presented either modulated or unmodulated, and you will answer whether the sound source for X was A or B.

First, choose one of the following three modulation conditions and press the start button. Immediately after that, music will be played three times. Next, select the bottom radio button A or B, and finally press the Send button. The answer will then be displayed.
